Courses we offer

Orthodontics & Dentofacial
Orthopaedics

Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopaedics

Our evidence-based Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics Postgraduate Diploma covers all orthodontic treatment techniques, treatment planning, digital orthodontics, cephalometry, and much more in a 12-month part-time programme, allowing you to enhance your orthodontic skills regardless of your experience level.

View course

Aesthetic & Restorative
Dentistry

Aesthetic & Restorative Dentistry

Enhance your dental career with our Aesthetic and Restorative Dentistry Postgraduate Diploma, designed to expand your expertise in contemporary restorative techniques, occlusion, digital dentistry, facial aesthetics, and more through a 12-month part-time programme delivered online in our Virtual Learning Environment.

View course

Dental Implantology &
Oral Surgery

Dental Implantology & Oral Surgery

Elevate your professional skill set through our Dental Implantology & Oral Surgery postgraduate programme, covering topics such as bone grafting, prosthetic solutions, scrubbing techniques, dental implantology, and complex dental extractions.

Frequently asked questions

Does the London Dental Institute offer in-person teaching?

While our courses are conducted online through our Virtual Learning Environment, we also provide regular in-person training opportunities where our global community comes together to enhance their knowledge and expand their professional network.

Can I spread the cost of studying at the London Dental Institute?

Yes. Although you can offer a one-time payment on our courses, we understand that might not be a suitable option for everyone which is why we offer monthly payment options, allowing you to spread the cost over the year of study.

What is the Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) at the London Dental Institute?

The VLE is the Virtual Learning Environment where you can learn your chosen course on an online platform, watch lectures, access course materials, receive tutor support and interact with fellow students.

The VLE operates on both desktop and on a mobile app allowing you to learn at home and on the go for a completely flexible and unique postgraduate dental learning experience.

Is London Dental Institute accredited?

Yes, the London Dental Institute is accredited as Level 7 by EduQual Level 7, a globally recognised UK qualifications regulator. This accreditation ensures that our Diplomas meet rigorous standards of quality and provide students with a reputable qualification that’s recognised internationally. Our Institute is committed to delivering a high-standard education that prepares dental professionals for successful careers in their chosen specialisms.

What are the benefits of online postgraduate programmes in dentistry?

Online postgraduate dentistry courses offer several benefits, including flexibility in study hours, the ability to continue working in clinical practice while studying, reduced travel and accommodation costs, access to international faculty and experts, and the opportunity to study from the comfort of your home or office.

Can international dental professionals enrol in online postgraduate dentistry courses?

Yes, online postgraduate dentistry courses are accessible to international dental professionals. These courses offer an opportunity for dental professionals from different countries to gain expert knowledge without the need for travel or relocation.

How are the practical aspects and clinical training handled in online courses by the LDi?

Many online dentistry courses incorporate practical aspects and clinical training through virtual simulations, hands-on workshops, and case discussions. Our Clinical Simulation Kit is instrumental in the development of your practical skills as well as being able to treat live patients throughout your course.
